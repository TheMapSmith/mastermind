A mastermind is a group of people who come together to reach a common goal.

Our goal:
Work together to build successful geospatial careers.

You can think of a mastermind group as a kind of like a professional show-and-tell. We meet regularly to discuss our success, share our failures, and get help from one another.

If you want to build a succesful career in the geospatial industry, it helps to be surrounded by inspirational people.

People who challenge you to learn and grow, who won't let you quit, and who will hold you accountable to yourself.


Stop tinkering alone and join a group to share your knowledge and learn from others like you in the geospatial industry.

We will bring our collective minds together in an honest, open, and inclusive way to help further our goal.

Being a better spatial professional.


Our group will focus on individual growth in the following areas.

Technical Abilities

Share your skills with the group and learn from others.

Business Skills

Learn the career tools your boss isn't teaching you.

Side Ventures

Increase your income by starting profitable side projects.

How it works

Daily Chats

We have a private chat group where you can ask for quick feedback, help, advice, guidance, or opinions.

Weekly One Hour Video Calls

These will be highly focused, agenda-driven, and time-limited chats where we all prepare our contributions ahead of time.

You should be ready to discuss:

  1. Your current project
  2. What is working well
  3. What you are struggling with
  4. One helpful resouce to share

Monthly Meetups (Where possible)

If you have fellow group members in your city or region, meet up with them for coffee or beer once a month and help each other out and form deeper connections.

Annual Master Meetings (Optional)

We'll find a centralized, affordable space for everyone to come together for a weekend and have an extra-long mastermind jam-session. Bring your story, highlight your successes, and share from your struggles on the stage. We'll pump out collaborative projects, host hackathons, mapathons, and lightning talks.

A mastermind is a powerful accountability tool, and gives us a platform to collaborate, share resources, support, and empower each other to do bigger and better things in the world.

The Spatial Mastermind will be a space for seeking advice from trusted advisors, bouncing ideas off each other, getting feedback from those with experience, receiving constructive criticism, building friendships, or finding business partnerships, a mentor, or other strategic relationships.
